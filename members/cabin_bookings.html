Cabin Booking Policy

  1. 1st January Bookings Open for:
    • Full Week bookings (7 days - Sunday to Sunday) for Members and Guests
    • 1st May Bookings Open for:
      • Shorter bookings (less than 7 days) for Members and Guests (including School Vacation periods)
    • A deposit of 30% is to be paid within 2 weeks of making the booking
    • Payment in Full is required at least 1 month prior to cabin stay
    • Bookings are not able to be amended, deposit is not refundable, the Member is responsible for all payments
    • Unused booked beds are charged at adult member rates
    • Lapsed bookings will be automatically removed from the website and deposit forfit (if you don't pay the deposit)
    • These rules will apply to all 2020 ski season bookings

Request a Booking

  1. Check the Booking Calendar for availability
  2. Fill out the online Booking Request Form
    (Please note that there are a maximum of 14 beds available in the Cabin at any one time)
  3. Your booking will be confirmed with an automated email from the Booking System and the Booking Calendar should show your new booking
  4. Pay a 30% deposit within 2 weeks of making the booking (See below for payment details)
  5. Pay the remaining amount 1 month before staying at the cabin
Email the Booking Officer(s) with any queries

Payment

Make seperate payments for each booking. Do not pay Subscription fees and cabin booking payments with a single transfer. It is hard enough trying to work out who the payment is from, let alone what they are paying for.
By Cheque
Send payment in full directly to the Treasurer (Georgina McCowan, 38 Emperor Drive Ocean Grove Vic, 3226) Please print out your confirmation email, or include your Booking Number with your payment. The Booking Number is also available on the website, just click on your name in the calendar.
Online
Use "Direct Debit" or "Pay Anyone" transfer using your online banking facility. Please include your full name, and your Booking Number in the transfer details.
You must then Email the Booking Officer(s) with the payment amount, Booking Number, and date of transfer so that it can be reconciled.
