Request a Booking

  1. Check Cabin Availability
  2. Email The Booking Officer edelweiss@arrowsreach.com (Bill Farrow) with the following details:
    • Your requested dates (clearly stating the day coming into and out of the cabin)
    • The number of Members in your booking
    • The number of Guests in your booking
    (Please note that there are a maximum of 14 beds available in the Cabin at any one time)

  3. Your booking will be confirmed with a return email - note that the cabin availability page may be a few days behind, with phone bookings or unconfirmed emails, so it is important for us to confirm your request. To check your bookings just look at the cabin availability details below.

  4. Send payment in full directly to the Treasurer (Francina Macdondald, 2 Allowah Terrace, Richmond, Vic 3121).

This page is open only to members with the password, but if you are still concerned about privacy, and do not want your name listed on the Calender, let us know in the email, and we will put your booking in as anonymous.
