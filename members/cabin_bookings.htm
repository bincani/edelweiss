Cabin Booking Policy

  1. Full Week (7 days - Sunday to Sunday) bookings to open 1st January, Shorter bookings open 1st May
  2. A deposit of 30% is to be paid within 2 weeks of making the booking
  3. Payment in Full is required at least 1 month prior to cabin stay
  4. Bookings are not able to be amended, deposit is not refundable, the Member is responsible for all payments
  5. Unused booked beds are charged at adult member rates
  6. Lapsed bookings will be automatically removed from the website and deposit forfit
  7. These rules will apply to all 2005 ski season bookings

Request a Booking

  1. Check Cabin Availability below
  2. Fill out the online Booking Request Form
    3. (Please note that there are a maximum of 14 beds available in the Cabin at any one time)

  3. Your booking will be confirmed with a return email from the Booking Officer - note that the cabin availability page may be a few days behind due to phone bookings or unconfirmed emails, so it is important for us to confirm your request.
    To check your bookings just look at the cabin availability details below.

  4. Email the Booking Officer edelweiss@arrowsreach.com (Bill Farrow) with any queries
  5. Once your booking has been confirmed, send payment in full directly to the Treasurer (Francina Macdondald, 2 Allowah Terrace, Richmond, Vic 3121).

This page is open only to members with the password, but if you are still concerned about privacy, and do not want your name listed on the Calender, let us know in an email, and we will put your booking in as anonymous.
