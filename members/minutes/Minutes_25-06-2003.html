Cabin Managers Report:

(Peter Smith. Report transcribed as follows)
  1. The Cabin is in good condition for the 2003 Snow Season.
  2. Work Party No.2 on 10 & 11 May 2003 attended to not only several small items left over from Work Party No.1 2003 but also repairs to damaged cable at rear of lodge. My thanks to Ranald, Bill, Ingrid, and Dorothy, for their support, expertise and of course their company.
  3. New Building opening/closing instructions are now applicable, my thanks to Dorothy.
  4. Annual fire inspection is complete and the plumber is up to date following removal of old stove, installation of BBQ and Heater Service.
  5. Queens Birthday weekend saw finalisation of a further few items, and some intensive repairs to the ductwork under lodge following wombat/rat damage.
  6. Slowly the lodge is becoming more vermin proof and progress baiting has been very effective.
  7. I have noticed unreported use of the lodge (unpaid for). Perhaps we need to consider a Security Patrol.
