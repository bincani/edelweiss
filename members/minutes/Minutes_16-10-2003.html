General Business:

  1. Allan Banfield has submitted a letter of resignation. Motion by Peter Smith to offer "Life Membership" to Allan Banfield, seconded by Hamish Farrow.
  2. Cabin Booking System
    1. Look at using Internet Direct Deposit facility.
    2. Discussion on requiring a booking deposit or full payment on booking.
    3. Use the webpage calendar to show booking status. ie use different colours for the booking entry to indicate unpaid, paid, or need people to take up beds that have already been paid for.
    4. The proposal to be considered at the next meeting is: Pay in full at time of booking, refundable up to 4 weeks prior to the booked date. After the cut off date, if you can't fill the beds you have paid for, it is up to the member to organise someone else to take over the beds (and not to involve the treasurer). This aims to maximise the usage of the cabin, and keep the treasure's jobs as simple as possible.
    5. Members contact details need to be made available on the web, including mobile phone numbers.
    6. Members priority over other members guests is currently 2 weeks. This should be changed to 4 weeks to bring it in line with the pre-payment cut-off.
    7. Do we need a "No Snow" policy ?
    8. A Decision on this proposal will be made at the next meeting. Please send any comments to the President or Secretary, and come and vote at the next meeting.
