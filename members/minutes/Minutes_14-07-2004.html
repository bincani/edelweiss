    • Proposed change to Lodge Operating Instructions
        • Some difficulty has been reported in lighting the heater during winter. Technical advice received is of the opinion that the gas regulator will gum up in cold conditions and if left shut down for extended periods. Accordingly, to avoid an expensive service call, I see no reason to continually shut down the heater during the ski season. The heater could be left in the pilot postion with the main gas valve on and the other gas valves off on lodge shutfdown. Any comment please ?
