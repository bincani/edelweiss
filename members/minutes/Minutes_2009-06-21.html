Ongoing business:

 

Sam Harry has relinquished his membership. This brought up the discussion about memberships being able to be transferred within families.

 

Edelweiss Ski Club operates under the rules of the Incorporated Associations Act. Clause 4 (10) of the Incorporated Associations Act Model Rules states:

 

(10) A right, privilege, or obligation of a person by reason of membership of the Association –

  1. a)is not capable of being transferred or transmitted to another person; and 

  2. b)terminates upon the cessation of membership whether by death or resignation or otherwise. 

 

6 or 7 years ago it was investigated whether the club could incorporate in a different manner, and if the redevelopment occurs then this should be looked into again, to weigh up implications between potentially changing tax status etc on one hand, and the need to be able to sell an individuals share to cover redevelopment costs.
