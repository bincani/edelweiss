Redevelopment  Update

Peri - Have engaged a building surveyor,  Stokes Perna, for advice and assessment of the site. This draft report will be forthcoming.

Re: Compliance with BCA – we can alter up to 50% without having to reassess the whole building for compliance. This requires less than 25% increase in floor space, but also structural integrity, and will also need a BCA report of existing building.  The structural adequacy of the existing building may be an issue.

The staged development currently proposed includes: Stage 1 – bathrooms then internal repositioning of bedrooms and Stage 2 - would not fit within a BCA compliance partial assessment.

Next step is an engineer/builder (one is at Dinner Plain) to assess the structural situation, and see if it is reasonable to rebuild or refurbish the cabin.

Moving  forward …

If the cabin is structurally sound then a refurbishment is an option, if not then a new structure will be required.   The most viable option will be costed, then the club will determine how we pay for this. Then proceed.
