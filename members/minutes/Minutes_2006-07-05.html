Presidents Report:

(Ranald McCowan)

  • Thankyou to those people who have worked on the cabin and club matters: cabin manager, the bookings officer, treasurer.

  • This has been a big year, significan cabin working parties, new members.

  • Future of the club, and the need to redevelop the cabin. The Financial review had a article on Alpine developments. There is less and less unencoumbered land for development. The Maritz developers were very happy to talk with us for perhaps a future development on out site (in about 3 years). There is another development group who built “Husky” at Falls Creek. We have to know what it is that we want to do. We will have to keep working on this, and should establish an ongoing subcommittee for this to keep continuity with the developers.

  • The finanial health of the club – we have to live within our means. This year we have spent more on things such as re-carpeting. We need to scale back on our expenditures, and increase our cabin stays.

  • People are making their bookings but are not paying their deposit, please pay your deposit on time.

Treasures Report:

(Judy McCowan)

  • Financial Report and Assets and Liabilities reports tabled.

  • This year had significant expenditure on cabin maintenance, which has been countered by new members joining.

  • Subscriptions and other payments via the internet must have at least your name in the transfer details.

  • Judy has not got the actual bank statements yet and is using the internet facility to view the accounts.

  • There has been an increase of about 6% in costs, and so we should increase the subscription fees. Member Subscriptions from $350 to $370, and Spouse/Junior from $50 to $60, work party levy from $30 to $50, and a late fees of $50.

  • Cabin usage has increased.

  • People have not been paying their cabin booking deposits, and we will need to send out invoices. Please pay by the end of August or a late fee will be levied. A 2nd notice will be sent out on the 1st September.

  • Proposal to add Judy to the signatory list for the bank accounts and reduce the requirements for signing cheques to a single signature. Proposed Sandy , 2nd Peter Smith..

Cabin Manager's Report:

(Peter Smith)

  • There have been no calls about the cabin, so it must be doing OK.

  • The heater is struggling, and stays on constantly when it's below 0 deg.

  • Still need to put in the vinel into the toilets and show stalls. The materials are all their.

  • There are two new toilets to be install

  • A small site at Mt Buller was sold for $400k

  • Propsal to purchase a washing machine (5kg) to be installed into the hot water room.

  • Propsal to purchase a clothes dryer to be installed into the “cold porch” room.

  • Need to rat proof the hot water room

  • Need an electrician to replace the lights in the bed rooms

  • Ingrid is happy to run some fundraising to buy a gas fired log fire.

  • Bi-annual Fire Inspection ($400) is now required by Hotham Management, we need this for insurance.

  • Ranald and Andrew met with the Maritz developers. Peter has sent our plans and geo-tec survey to them. The expensive suites with no views are not selling, we should consider lower cost studios for the side with now views. The prices at our end of the mountain have almost equalised with the villiage end of the mountain.
